Meet Dr. Bhavin Doshi

Board Certified Pediatrician

Dr. Bhavin Doshi—fondly known as "Dr. D" by his patients—has been practicing pediatrics for over 15 years and values the uniqueness of every child. His relaxed, child-friendly approach makes visits fun and engaging, helping to build strong, trusting relationships with families. He believes in empowering not only parents but also children by encouraging them to take an active role in their care—whether describing their symptoms or repeating treatment plans in their own words.

Dr. Doshi enjoys connecting with children by learning about their friends, hobbies, and school, which helps him understand their world and provide care that feels personal. His special interests include managing chronic conditions such as asthma and allergies, as well as guiding families through concerns about growth and development.

As a parent of two, Dr. Doshi understands the worries families face, whether a child is well or unwell, and is committed to providing compassionate, personalized care for children of all ages.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is DocToc?
DocToc is our virtual AI assistant. We encourage you to start by using DocToc to independently address your concerns using strategies directly approved and recommended by Dr. Doshi. However, Dr. Doshi is always available to consult with you directly whenever you feel the need.
Are you currently accepting new patients?
Yes! We are happy to welcome new families to our practice. We accept patients from newborn through adolescence. Please contact our office to schedule your first visit.
Do you offer same-day sick appointments?
We understand that children don't get sick on a schedule. We set aside time every day specifically for acute illnesses. If your child is sick, please call early in the day, and we will do our best to accommodate you.
What should I bring to my child's first visit?
Please arrive 15 minutes early and bring your child's up-to-date immunization records, any previous medical records, your current insurance card, and a valid photo ID.
